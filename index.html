The steady decline of the South African state over decades has prompted the emergence of parallel institutions and community organisations working to fill the vacuum of power and address the failure of crucial infrastructure, coupled with one of the highest Bitcoin adoption rates globally.
If you’re curious to learn how Bitcoin fixes this, then this is a conference you’ll not want to miss.
