LIVE: Adopting Bitcoin Cape Town 2026 | Machankura Stage Day 1
HOSTED BY

For the third year running, Adopting Bitcoin Cape Town 2026 continues to champion Bitcoin adoption, empowering individuals, and nurturing parallel institutions and economies to reshape South Africa’s financial landscape. As the state’s decline leaves critical infrastructure gaps, and with growing Bitcoin adoption, this conference is a must-attend to explore how Bitcoin helps people build resilient systems, replacing the failed centralized system.
